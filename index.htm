About

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Marketing at the David Eccles School of Business, University of Utah. My research focuses on digital platforms, innovation, crowdfunding, prosocial products, and fairness in businesses.

I examine information in unstructured data (text, visual) and understand its effects on consumer decision-making and its impact on businesses. My research approach is to employ cutting-edge techniques in machine learning and deep learning to construct measures in large-scale data and analyze them using econometric methods while applying marketing and behavioral theories to understand the underlying phenomena.
